Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of the organization?

Our mission is to provide support and resources to the people of Palestine, focusing on humanitarian aid, education, and advocacy.

How can I contribute to the cause?

You can contribute by donating, volunteering, or spreading awareness about our initiatives and the situation in Palestine.

Where do the donations go?

Donations are allocated to various projects including medical aid, educational programs, and community development initiatives in Palestine.

What are the main areas of focus for your projects?

Our projects primarily focus on healthcare, education, and community development to improve the quality of life for Palestinians.

Are there any upcoming events I can participate in?

We regularly organize events and campaigns. Please check our events page or contact us for more information on how to get involved.

Why are we doing this to support Palestine?

We are committed to supporting Palestine because we believe in the fundamental rights to freedom, justice, and equality for all people. Our efforts aim to alleviate suffering, promote education, and empower communities to build a better future.
